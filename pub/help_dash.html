• The dashboard will show any assets that have been "tagged". This is a convenient way to track assets currently being used.
  • Cloning a tagged asset will cause the cloned asset to be tagged as well. Assets created by clicking the "new" button in an asset category tab will also be automatically tagged.
  • Assets can be untagged at any time by selecting their checkboxe(s) and clicking the "Untag" button.
  • Searching multiple categories is also performed in the dashboard. Click the "Multi-category search" button, and then select the categories you would like to search. (Control or shift-clicking will allow you to select multiple categories). Fields that are present in all the selected categories may then be searched.
